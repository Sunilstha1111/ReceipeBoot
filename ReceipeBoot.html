Breakfast Receipe for Potato Pancake

Masterchef for Today: Sunil Shrestha

For me this is the breakfast to have in the morning because it is very tasty,
refreshes my whole body and makes my morining delightful. I try to make it everyday,
but it takes a little bit of time to prepare, so I usually make it every other
day.


Ingredients needed

  • 4 cups shredded peeled potatoes (about 4 large potatoes)
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon grated onion
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • Oil for frying
  • Chopped parsley, applesauce and sour cream, optional

Directions

  1. Rinse shredded potatoes in cold water; drain well, squeezing to remove excess water.
  2. Place in a large bowl, and stir in egg, flour, onion, salt and pepper.
  3. In a large nonstick skillet, heat 1/4 in. of oil over medium heat.
  4. Working in batches, drop potato mixture by 1/3 cupfuls into oil; press to flatten slightly.
  5. Fry both sides until golden brown; drain on paper towels.
  6. Serve immediately and if desired, sprinkle with parsley and top with applesauce and sour cream.

Yield: 6 servings
